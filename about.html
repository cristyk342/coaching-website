About Liam

Building clarity, confidence, and direction — one conversation at a time.

Why I Started Coaching

I didn't step into coaching because everything in my life was perfect — I stepped into it because I learned, firsthand, what it feels like to be stuck, overlooked, and unsure of what comes next. For years, I worked hard, built a strong career, and showed up for everyone around me… but I also realized how easy it is to pour into others while letting your own direction fade into the background.

At one point, I had to take a hard look at my life — my health, my career, my finances, and my peace — and be honest about what wasn't serving me anymore. That moment changed everything. I committed to rebuilding from the inside out, developing stronger habits, taking ownership of my personal growth, and creating a life that felt aligned with who I was becoming, not just who I had been.

Through that journey, I kept meeting people who were struggling with the same challenges:

  • Feeling lost.
  • Feeling unsupported.
  • Feeling like they were doing everything "right," but still not moving forward.

I realized that the things I had learned — not just professionally, but personally — were exactly what others needed. Coaching wasn't just a career pivot for me; it was a calling. I wanted to build something that helped real people make real progress, without judgment, without "guru" energy, and without pretending life doesn't get messy sometimes.

Liam's Direction was born from that mission.

My Goal is Simple

Help people gain clarity, confidence, and control over their lives — personally, professionally, and financially.

Because when you understand your direction, everything else becomes possible.

And I'm here to walk that journey with you, step by step.
